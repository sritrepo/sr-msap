About the Role

Sphere Rocket VA connects talented Virtual Assistants with US-based clients across real estate, e-commerce, and business services. We're always looking for skilled, reliable, and driven VAs ready to make an impact remotely.

What You'll Do
  • Provide specialized or administrative support to assigned US clients
  • Manage tasks aligned to your applied role (admin, social media, cold calling, CRM, etc.)
  • Communicate clearly and consistently via Slack, Zoom, and email
  • Deliver work on time and report progress to your client
Requirements
  • At least 1 year of VA or related remote work experience
  • Excellent written and spoken English communication
  • Reliable high-speed internet (minimum 25 Mbps) with backup connection
  • Own working PC or Mac
  • Ability to work US business hours
